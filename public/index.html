Principles

The standards behind every decision.

A clear operating model for how products are designed, built, and shipped.

  1. 01
    Clarity Design should make purpose immediately understood. If something requires explanation, it is not finished.
  2. 02
    Reduction We remove the unnecessary so the essential can speak. Less is not a style; it is discipline.
  3. 03
    Integrity No decoration without meaning. Every element must justify its existence through function.
  4. 04
    Continuity Experiences should feel coherent across time, screen, and context. Familiar, not repetitive.
  5. 05
    Precision Details are not embellishments. They are where trust is built.
  6. 06
    Longevity We design for use over years, not attention over minutes.
  7. 07
    Human Scale Interfaces must respect attention, reduce effort, and never compete with the user’s intent.
  8. 08
    Honesty No dark patterns. No artificial urgency. The product should behave exactly as it appears.
  9. 09
    Restraint Strong design does not try to impress. It works quietly and lets outcomes speak.
  10. 10
    Craft Thorough to the last pixel, the last transition, the last state.
